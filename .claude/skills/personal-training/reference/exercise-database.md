## Substitution Guide

**No pull-up bar?**
- Pull-ups → Inverted Rows (under table) or Band Pulldowns

**No dumbbells?**
- Goblet Squat → Bodyweight Squat with tempo
- DB Row → Inverted Row
- DB Press → Push-up variations

**Joint-friendly swaps:**
- Barbell Squat → Leg Press or Goblet Squat
- Deadlift → Hip Thrust or Glute Bridge
- Overhead Press → Landmine Press

## Tempo Notation

Format: Eccentric-Pause-Concentric-Pause

| Tempo | Meaning | Best For |
|-------|---------|----------|
| 2-0-2-0 | 2s down, no pause, 2s up | Strength |
| 3-1-1-0 | 3s down, 1s pause, 1s up | Hypertrophy |
| 1-0-X-0 | 1s down, explosive up | Power |
| 4-2-1-0 | Slow eccentric | Control/Rehab |
