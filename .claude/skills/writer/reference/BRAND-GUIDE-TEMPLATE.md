# Brand Guide Template

**Version:** 1.0
**Purpose:** Define your authentic voice, mission, and visual standards for content creation

---

## How to Use This Template

**This is YOUR brand guide.** Copy this template to `BRAND-GUIDE.md` and customize it to reflect:

1. **Your mission** - Why you create content, who you serve
2. **Your voice** - How you communicate (professional, casual, technical, accessible)
3. **Your principles** - What you stand for, what you avoid
4. **Your visual style** - Consistent imagery that represents your brand

The `/write` command and writer skill use this guide to maintain consistency across all your content.

## Quality Checklist

**Before publishing, verify:**

**Voice:**
- [ ] Correct voice mode used
- [ ] Consistent with brand principles
- [ ] Accessible to target audience

**Content:**
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Original insight (not just aggregation)
- [ ] Sources cited properly

**Standards:**
- [ ] Follows your mandatory rules
- [ ] Avoids your anti-patterns

**Visual:**
- [ ] Hero image matches brand style
- [ ] Diagrams are clear and consistent
