## Earth Clinic Top Protocols

### Ted's Alkalizing Formulas

**Basic Baking Soda:**
- 1/4 tsp baking soda in 8oz water
- 2x daily on empty stomach
- pH target: 7.0-7.5

**Lemon/Lime + Baking Soda:**
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon/lime juice
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- Wait for fizzing to stop
- Add 8oz water
- 2x daily

**ACV + Baking Soda:**
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 8oz water
- 2-3x daily

### Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV)

**Standard Protocol:**
- 1-2 tbsp raw, unfiltered ACV
- 8oz water
- Optional: 1 tsp honey
- Before meals or morning/evening

**Uses:** Digestion, blood sugar, weight management, skin tonic

### Borax Protocol

**Men's Formula:**
- 1/8 tsp borax (20 Mule Team)
- 1 liter water
- Sip throughout day
- 5 days on, 2 days off

**Women's Formula:**
- 1/16 tsp borax
- 1 liter water
- Same schedule

**Claimed uses:** Boron supplementation, joint health, hormone balance

### Oil Pulling

**Method:**
- 1 tbsp coconut or sesame oil
- Swish vigorously 15-20 minutes
- Morning, before eating/drinking
- Spit into trash (not drain)
- Rinse mouth, brush teeth

### Hydrogen Peroxide (External)

**Wound Care:**
- 3% H2O2 for cleaning wounds
- Don't use on deep or serious wounds

**Mouthwash:**
- Equal parts 3% H2O2 and water
- Swish 30-60 seconds
- Don't swallow

**Ear Drops:**
- Few drops 3% H2O2 in ear
- Let bubble for 5-10 minutes
- Drain onto tissue
