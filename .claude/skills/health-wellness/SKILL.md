## Homeopathic Fundamentals

### Potency Guide

| Potency | Strength | Use Case |
|---------|----------|----------|
| 6X, 6C | Low | Acute physical symptoms, frequent dosing |
| 12X, 12C | Medium-Low | General acute conditions |
| 30X, 30C | Medium | Standard acute/chronic use |
| 200C | High | Constitutional, less frequent |
| 1M+ | Very High | Professional use only |

**X vs C Potencies:**
- X (Decimal): 1:10 dilution ratio
- C (Centesimal): 1:100 dilution ratio
- Higher number = more diluted = stronger (homeopathic principle)

### Dosing Principles

**Acute Conditions:**
- Dose every 15-30 minutes initially
- Reduce frequency as symptoms improve
- Stop when symptoms resolve

**Chronic Conditions:**
- Less frequent dosing (1-3x daily)
- Allow time between doses
- Often higher potencies (30C+)

**General Rules:**
- Let pellets dissolve under tongue
- Avoid food/drink 15 min before/after
- Avoid strong flavors (mint, coffee) during treatment
- Stop when better (don't continue unnecessarily)

## Earth Clinic Protocols (Ted's Remedies)

### Alkalizing Protocol
**Purpose:** Balance body pH, reduce acidity

**Basic Protocol:**
- 1/4 tsp baking soda in 8oz water
- 2x daily on empty stomach
- Monitor urine pH (target 7.0-7.5)

**Lemon/Lime Variation:**
- 2 tbsp lemon juice + 1/2 tsp baking soda
- Wait for fizzing to stop
- Add 8oz water
- Take 2x daily

### Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV)
**General Tonic:**
- 1-2 tbsp ACV in 8oz water
- Add honey if desired
- Take before meals

**Uses:** Digestion, blood sugar support, skin tonic

### Borax Protocol (Ted's)
**Note:** Controversial - research thoroughly before use

**Purpose:** Mineral supplementation (boron)

**Protocol:**
- 1/8 tsp borax in 1 liter water (men)
- 1/16 tsp borax in 1 liter water (women)
- Sip throughout day
- 5 days on, 2 days off

**Claimed uses:** Joint health, hormone balance, detox

### Oil Pulling
**Method:**
- 1 tbsp coconut or sesame oil
- Swish 15-20 minutes (morning, before eating)
- Spit out (not down drain)
- Rinse mouth, brush teeth

**Purpose:** Oral health, detoxification

### Hydrogen Peroxide Therapy
**External uses:**
- 3% H2O2 for wound cleaning
- Diluted for mouthwash (1:1 with water)
- Ear drops (few drops, let bubble)

**Note:** Internal use controversial - research thoroughly

## Safety Guidelines

### Always Refer to Doctor For:
- Chest pain or difficulty breathing
- Severe or worsening symptoms
- High fever (>103°F adults)
- Symptoms in infants/young children
- Pregnancy-related concerns
- Mental health emergencies
- Any condition requiring emergency care

### Interaction Warnings
- Research herb-drug interactions
- Inform doctors of all supplements
- Be cautious combining protocols
- Start low, go slow with new remedies

### Quality Considerations
- Source remedies from reputable suppliers
- Check expiration dates
- Store properly (away from strong odors, electronics)
- Research manufacturer reputation
